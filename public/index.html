How Border Walk works

Each day everyone gets the same start and target country. Type a neighboring country to cross into it — you can only move to a country that shares a land border with where you currently are. Keep hopping until you reach the target.

Try to get there in as few hops as possible: the fewer hops beyond the shortest possible route, the better your walk.

Scoring

Your score starts at 100 and drops 10 points for every hop beyond the optimal route. Any successful arrival scores at least 10. Giving up scores 0. Hints don't change your score, but are noted alongside your result.

Rules & conventions

Each day everyone gets the same start and target country. Type a neighboring country to cross into it — you can only move to a country that shares a land border with where you currently are — and try to reach the target in as few hops as possible.

Scoring: your score starts at 100 and drops 10 points for every hop beyond the optimal route. Any successful arrival scores at least 10. Giving up scores 0. Hints don't change your score, but are noted alongside your result.

Move only between countries that share a land border. Island nations without land borders sit out for now. Kaliningrad counts for Russia; Ceuta & Melilla connect Spain to Morocco; Nakhchivan connects Azerbaijan to Turkey. The daily route is the same for every player.
